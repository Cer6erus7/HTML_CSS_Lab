Article 01

One fascinating fact about the Roman Empire is that they had a sophisticated system of public baths called "Thermae." These bathhouses were not only for hygiene but also served as social centers where people gathered to relax, exercise, and socialize. The largest and most famous of these bathhouses, the Baths of Diocletian, could accommodate up to 3,000 people at a time and featured amenities such as libraries, art galleries, and gardens alongside the bathing facilities. These Thermae were integral to Roman culture and society, reflecting their emphasis on communal well-being and leisure activities.

A fun fact about the Golden State Warriors is that they hold the record for the best regular-season record in NBA history. During the 2015-2016 season, the Warriors finished with a remarkable 73-9 record, surpassing the previous record held by the 1995-1996 Chicago Bulls who finished with a 72-10 record.
